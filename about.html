About Us

Welcome to Book Donors Nepal, a revolutionary platform that connects book donors with those in need. Our mission is to help students and individuals from all fields gain access to the books they need by providing a simple and efficient way to donate and find books.

By signing up and logging in, donors can easily list the books they wish to give away, and seekers can search for and request the books they need. Our goal is to create a community where knowledge is shared freely and no book goes unused.

Join us in our mission to promote education and knowledge-sharing across Nepal. Together, we can make a difference, one book at a time.
